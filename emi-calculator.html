What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly amount paid by a borrower to repay a loan. It includes both principal and interest components.

How is EMI Calculated?

EMI is calculated using a reducing balance formula that considers loan amount, interest rate, and loan tenure in months.

EMI Formula

EMI = P × R × (1+R)^N / ((1+R)^N – 1)
